Background

Motivation

This is a project I completed during my employee at Smart Home Company in 2019. As a marketing manager, I am also responsible for design work. The company is undergoing reorganization, splitting the product R&D department, and changing it to outsourcing ODM, and at the same time completing the upgrade and expansion of the sales product line. The company needs to revise and innovate the product map, product brochure, official account, and company website. Therefore, this project is based on the result of completion under brand-new design elements.

The above content has been completed, but unfortunately, it has not been put into use for too long. The company was dissolved due to funding problems three months after the completion of the project.

Project upgrade

After the course, I committed to exploring the smart home. I decided to focus on the platform, including iPadOS and macOS.

Goals

Original idea

In the initial planning of the website development, there were several clear goals:

  • Only as a static display website, the company's main operating direction is still offline communication, relying on the sales staff to provide a quotation after on-site measurement. No manpower is allocated online, and no real-time interactive design is done.
  • Compared with the previous version, which is only a product display, this version must increase the company's development history, service, cases, and business cooperation. My idea about the position of the website is information dissemination, so I created an additional page to explain smart homes and technology.
  • Product display must be improved in the previous version with only pictures and parameters written, application descriptions must be added, and colors should be enriched as much as possible.
Goal of upgrade

After undergoing complete UX/UI and front-end development training, I reviewed this website again. The upgrade will improve the following:

  • Complete the responsive design and optimize the layout for the small screen.
  • Icon adjustments to improve the problem of inconsistent icon styles.
  • Translated and presented in English instead.
  • The presentation of the product table is adjusted to omit the less important information. For multi-color products, visual presentation.

Wireframs

Trio-section structure

The design of the website is divided into three sections: Smart Home, Solution, and About. Smart Home focuses on the introduction of the theory and does not involve products and solutions. After all, smart home is not a very mature and well-known field, the provision of knowledge can also make the website less utilitarian.

The Solution section is classified according to application scenarios, and the products are displayed one by one.

In the About section, in addition to the company's basic development history, it also lists cases to strengthen the company's service and business cooperation capabilities.

Duo-navigation layout

The Trio-section structure navigation bar is not enough to make the navigation of the website more precise, so the second level navigation bar is added.

The drop-down secondary navigation bar will become too long due to too many split elements. In the end, the two-column arrangement is adopted after referring to the design of Apple's official website, supplemented by icons to deepen the visualization.

Accurate product presentation

The products are presented side by side with labels in the front view. The product information is unified and integrated below on each page of the Solution and presented in a table. The table does not carry all the parameters of the product. The product name, material, and color are retained. Not showing the price is to reserve the negotiation space for offline sales, as long as the internal personnel of the installation information such as the size, installation method, and voltage.

Style

Icons

A very important part of this project upgrade is the unification of the icons. In the initial revision, Smart Home and About are filled, and the solution part continues the stroke style of the product catalog. The upgrade is unified with color filling, and almost all icons have been redesigned. The icon uses rounded corners extensively, and the size is 24px.

Products’ image

The product images continue the design at the beginning of the revision. At that time, I first tried actual shooting but considering that it is not easy to unify the size of the products in flow-up editing, and the finishing of the products after shooting also takes a lot of time. There are more than 50 products of different sizes. Finally, I drew the products in vector. The product is as realistic as possible in terms of proportions, materials, fonts, reflections, and shadows.

Responsive web design

The biggest goal of this upgrade is to complete the responsive design. It does not have many visual changes in layout, main adjustments are in font size, spacing, tables, etc. The picture is scaled to the most suitable size.

Hover's animation

Purely static web pages lack dynamic elements, so hover animation is widely added. Animation is about color changes, not only in the navigation bar but also in many icons and text link elements on the content page.

Dark & light background

The background color of the page adopts a staggered layout, and the dark background is used on the pages with more products or images. The main reason is that the products are mostly white, and the dark colors are more prominent for products display; while the pages with more text maintain a white background. It's clean and concise.
